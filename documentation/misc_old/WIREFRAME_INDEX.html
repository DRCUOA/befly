UX Wireframes

A progressive reading experience design system exemplar

Design Philosophy

Progressive Disclosure

Interface elements fade away as engagement deepens, creating space for focused attention.

Respectful Attention

No interruptions, no forced interactions, no artificial urgency. The design honors the reader's time.

Temporal Awareness

Design acknowledges the passage of time and allows for natural reading rhythms.
